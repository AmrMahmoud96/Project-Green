Our Mission

Alpha Factory aims to reduce the stigma around investing and make it accessible to everyone. Ultimately, Alpha Factory aims to close the world’s investment gap by making investing part of everyday life!

Alpha Factory aims to use our platform and proprietary portfolio generation techniques to help the average person manage their own investments. Specifically, we aim to get to know you through our quick questionnaire, so we can create a personalized and diversified portfolio of assets at a risk tolerance you are comfortable with while meeting your long-term investment goals. From there, we manage your money for you – this includes automatically rebalancing your portfolio to account for any market fluctuations and reinvesting your investment proceeds to ensure that your money doesn’t take any breaks while working for you. We also understand that investment goals change over time and we offer dynamic portfolio modifications to address your evolving user preferences and inputs.
