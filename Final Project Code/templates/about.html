Portfolios as Unique as You!

To create a tailored portfolio for each investor, Alpha Factory considers the return goals and risk-tolerance of each of our investors.

Everyone is different and has different investment goals. Often times investors do not even know what their investment goals are, and if they do, it’s hard to put them in numbers/words. Alpha Factory tries to help with that. Specifically, we try to match return goals to life goals by considering what our clients are saving for. As such our return goals are largely catered to the following broad categories of life goals:

  • Short-term (~5 to 10 years): saving to purchase a car/home
  • Medium- term (~10 to 20 years): creating an education fund for your children
  • Long-term (20+ years): saving for retirement

Finally, our unique portfolio generation techniques depend on the risk-tolerance level of our investors. Rather than overfitting with a continuous spectrum of return goals and risk constraints, Alpha Factory created five distinct portfolios to accommodate the different risk-tolerance levels of our customers. We find this strategy better fits our mission as it helps lower fees.

To determine the return goals and risk-tolerance of our investors we require them to fill out a quick questionnaire when joining our platform. Find your risk-tolerance level by joining us today!

Investment Strategy

Inspired by the success of proven asset managers such as Bridgewater Associates and AQR Investments, Alpha Factory uses a Risk Parity portfolio generation strategy.

Risk Parity portfolios rely on risk-based diversification to generate higher and more consistent returns. Since Risk Parity portfolios begin with lower exposure to equities relative to traditional portfolios, the risk of the portfolio is spread more evenly across other asset classes. With Alpha Factory striving to create portfolios that perform well in all financial environments the Risk Parity approach is critical. In general, equities do well in high-growth and low-inflation environments; bonds do well in deflationary or recessionary environments; and commodities tend to perform best during inflationary environments, so having balanced exposure to these three main asset classes allows us to produce more consistent returns over time.

As an added measure for downside risk protection, Alpha Factory overlays a trend following system onto all their portfolios. This strategy has been proven to reduce major portfolio drawdowns and improve the long-term portfolio performance.
